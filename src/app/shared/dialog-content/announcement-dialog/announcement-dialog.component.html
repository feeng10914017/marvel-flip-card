Directions

Flip is a timed card memory game. Click the marvel cards to see what hero they uncover and try to find the matching hero underneath the other cards. Uncover two matching heros at once to eliminate them from the game. Eliminate all cards as fast as you can to win the game.

After pressing the start button, you have 30 seconds to match eight groups of heroes. Good luck!
